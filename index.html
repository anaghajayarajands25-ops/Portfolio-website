About

Biology meets Intelligence

I have always been fascinated by how technology can be used to understand the natural world. My studies focus on using data-driven methods to solve complex biological challenges. I believe that the combination of biology and artificial intelligence holds the key to future medical breakthroughs.

In my coursework, I learn how to analyze large datasets to find meaningful patterns that others might miss. I am particularly interested in how machine learning can speed up the process of drug discovery and development. My goal is to use my programming skills to help doctors provide better and more personalized treatments for their patients.

I aim to bridge the gap between computer science and laboratory research, believing that a collaborative approach between these two fields is essential for true innovation. Every day, I am motivated by the possibility of making a positive impact on global health.
